About Us


Welcome to hardCoded, your go-to destination for perfecting your resume and cover letter. Our mission is to provide tools and insights that help job seekers enhance their applications and secure more opportunities. Our team is dedicated to bringing you the best resources and support in your job search journey.


Our platform offers a range of features, including resume scanning, cover letter reviews, and personalized referral letter generation. We believe in the power of a well-crafted resume and cover letter to make a strong impression on potential employers.


At hardCoded, we value quality and strive to offer the best user experience. Whether you are just starting your job search or looking to refine your existing applications, we are here to help you every step of the way.

Our Approach


  • Expert Analysis

    Our tools utilize advanced algorithms to analyze your resume and cover letter, providing you with actionable insights and recommendations.


  • Personalized Feedback

    Receive tailored feedback that addresses the specific needs of your application, helping you make improvements that align with industry standards.


  • Continuous Improvement

    We are committed to continuously enhancing our platform based on user feedback and the latest trends in the job market.
